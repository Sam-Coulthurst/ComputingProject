Conventions:

    Variables:
        variables are generally named with the convention dimension_object

        e.g. magnitude of distance between earth and moon is d_moon_earth
             vector from earth and rocket is r_rocket_earth
             mass of moon is m_moon

    Functions:
        functions have documentation at the top. 
        '''
        First sentence is a summary

        Inputs:
            input1 (units) - (shape/type) - description

        Outputs:
            output1 (units) - (shape/type) - description
        '''
        
    Equations:
        Leave spaces between each number or operation
        eg. 2 * np.pi()
